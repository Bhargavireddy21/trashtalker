There are multiple waste management strategies and methods available. These strategies can be combined or rearranged to form a waste management system that fits an organization.

Modern waste management strategies are geared towards sustainability Other alternatives for waste management is to reduce, reuse and recycle waste.

Also known as physical reprocessing, recycling is ideal for the disposal of inorganic waste such as plastic, glass, and metals. Though organic waste such as paper and food can also be recycled, composting would be a better waste disposal method as it converts organic waste into nutrient-rich fertilizer.
