The trash talker is an waste management system which helps in recycling the waste

INTRODUCTION

Trash talker is a sensor-based waste management system. It is used to detect and process different types of waste further into simpler units. It collects waste like wet, dry (paper, plastic), wood, metal, glass, medical. It is even used to extract pure liquid from wet waste. It is an interactive bin. Its main motive is to avoid improper disposal of waste. The competitors in this field are quite a few and there is no proper discarding of waste.
